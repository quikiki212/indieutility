Frequently asked questions


Is my video uploaded to a server?
No. Conversion runs inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your file is not uploaded to IndieUtility.


Why is video conversion slower than image conversion?
Video encoding is computationally expensive. Browser-based FFmpeg is private and convenient, but large videos may take time.


Can this convert videos to GIF?
Yes. Choose GIF as the output format. Short clips work best because GIF files can become very large.


What formats are supported?
Output formats include MP4, WebM, GIF, MP3 and WAV. Input support depends on browser and FFmpeg WebAssembly support.
