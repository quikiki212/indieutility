How to Format and Clean YouTube Transcripts

1 Fetch Automatically using video URL

Simply paste the URL of your target video (e.g., watch links, youtu.be shares, or vertical YouTube Shorts) in the URL fetcher tab. Our client-side pipeline will pull raw subtitle tracks directly from open-CORS edge endpoints, completely bypassing manual interaction.

2 Stitch rolling word duplications

By turning on the "Deduplicate Rolling Captions" filter, the utility scans sentences word-by-word, analyzes matching word slices at transition points, and removes duplicated rolling sequences.

3 Format and Download

Once text is populated, select your custom options. Strip out repetitive timestamps or speaker names, fix low-case sentence flows, structure lines into standard reading paragraphs, and export the file as a clean `.txt` document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does YouTube repeat sentences in its transcript?

YouTube auto-generated subtitle lines overlap because they are designed as dynamic rolling blocks for on-screen readability. This tool's algorithm detects and automatically stitches these overlapping lines.

Can I remove timestamps automatically?

Yes. This formatting utility recognizes and isolates numerical time patterns (such as brackets, hours, minutes, and milliseconds) and cleanses them instantly.

Does this support multi-lingual texts?

Yes. By changing the language code parameter in the URL fetcher interface, you can fetch German, Spanish, French, Japanese, or any other supported native captions.

Is my data secure?

Completely. Processing runs inside your personal browser window. No transcripts, URLs, or modified files are uploaded, stored, or sent to external servers.
