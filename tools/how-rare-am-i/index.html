FAQ

Is my data uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything is calculated locally in your browser and stays on your device.

How accurate are these percentiles?

They are built from realistic local datasets and statistical estimates. They are useful for comparison, not official demographic reporting.

Why does country matter so much?

Countries differ in age structure, income levels, average height and typical shoe sizes, so the same number can rank differently depending on location.

What does the rarity badge mean?

Common means your profile is near the middle of the population. Extremely Rare means several of your traits sit far away from the middle.
