Frequently asked questions

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, AVIF, and ICO. Support varies by browser for some formats.

Can I resize images?

Yes. Enter a new width and the height will scale proportionally. Leave blank to keep the original size.
