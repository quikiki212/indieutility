Frequently asked questions

Is my regex and test string private?

Yes. All regex testing happens entirely in your browser—nothing is uploaded or stored.

What do the flags do?

g = global (all matches), i = case-insensitive, m = multiline, s = dotall, y = sticky, u = unicode.

Can I see capture groups?

Yes. When your pattern includes parentheses for capture groups, they'll be highlighted and displayed in the match details.
