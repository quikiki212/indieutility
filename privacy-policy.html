Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Introduction

IndieUtility ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and your rights as a user of our website and services.

2. Client-Side Processing

All IndieUtility tools process data entirely on your device using your web browser. We do not:

  • Collect or store your input data on our servers
  • Send your files or content to external servers
  • Use cloud storage or third-party APIs for processing
  • Retain copies of your documents or media

Everything you enter into our tools remains on your computer. We have no access to your data.

3. Information We Do Collect

We collect only minimal, non-personal information to improve our services:

  • Web server logs: Standard HTTP request logs including IP addresses, timestamps, and page requests
  • Analytics: Aggregate usage statistics to understand which tools are popular
  • Cookies: Session cookies for website functionality (optional, can be disabled)
  • Browser information: User agent and browser type for technical support

We do not collect: email addresses, names, location data, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through contact forms.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For serving advertisements. Google may use cookies and collect data according to their privacy policy
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): For fast asset loading
  • Web hosting: For site infrastructure

We recommend reviewing Google's AdSense Privacy Policy and other third-party privacy policies for complete information about their data practices.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Session management and user preferences
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Analytics (through Google Analytics or similar services)
  • Advertising through Google AdSense

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at indieutility@outlook.com .

7. Security

We take security seriously. Since data processing happens on your device:

  • Your data is not transmitted across the internet
  • We use HTTPS for all website connections
  • Server infrastructure is protected with industry-standard security
  • We regularly audit our code and practices

However, no system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at indieutility@outlook.com.

8. Children's Privacy

IndieUtility does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
